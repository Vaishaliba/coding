About Us

Avadh Enterprise is a leading fire protection company providing comprehensive services across Gujarat. The company specializes in designing, installing, and maintaining various fire fighting systems to ensure complete fire safety for buildings, factories, warehouses, offices, colleges, schools, hospitals, and more. With over 40 successful projects completed across diverse sectors, Avadh Enterprise is committed to delivering the highest standards of fire safety and protection through cutting-edge technologies and innovative solutions. The company has a passionate team of over 25 employees.

Products

Avadh Enterprise offers a comprehensive range of cutting-edge fire protection solutions, including fire hydrant systems, fire suppression systems, fire sprinkler systems, fire detection and alarm systems, and fire extinguisher systems. Our team of experts designs tailored fire safety systems for a wide range of industries, from automotive and manufacturing to healthcare and education, ensuring the highest standards of protection for lives, assets, and businesses. With state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities, an extensive R&D center, and a dedicated training academy, we are committed to delivering innovative technologies and exceptional service to safeguard our clients against fire hazards across Gujarat.
